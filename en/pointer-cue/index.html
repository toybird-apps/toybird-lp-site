Frequently asked questions

Before you start

How is Pointer Cue different from a full annotation tool?

A full annotation tool is suited to drawing and detailed markup. Pointer Cue is optimized for instantly pointing and framing without entering a drawing mode

Will I be charged automatically after the Windows trial?

No. The app is not purchased automatically when the 30-day trial ends. To continue, purchase the $0.99 version from Microsoft Store

How long can I use the limited Mac version?

It is available through December 31, 2026, with no automatic charge afterward

What is the difference between the limited Mac build and the App Store version?

Core features are the same. The direct build expires at the end of 2026, while the $3.99 App Store version has no expiration

Does it work with Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet?

Yes. Pointer Cue appears in shared-screen output and can be used in common online meetings and product demos

Does it record keystrokes or screen content?

No. It does not collect, store, or transmit typed content or screen information
