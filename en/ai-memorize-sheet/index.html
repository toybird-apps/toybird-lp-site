Frequently asked questions

What to know before you start

Can I use it on both iPhone and iPad?

Yes. It supports iPhone and iPad running iOS or iPadOS 16 or later, including PDF and multi-image import

What kinds of materials can I use?

You can use textbooks, worksheets, notes, workbooks, certification materials, screenshots, and PDFs. Recognition works best with printed text and may vary with lighting, tilt, complex tables, or handwriting

Can I edit automatically generated masks?

Yes. You can remove unwanted masks, add terms to hide, adjust mask density, and switch to another pattern

Are my materials sent outside the device?

No. Text recognition and term extraction are processed on device, and material images or recognized text are not sent to external AI services or Toybird Labs servers
